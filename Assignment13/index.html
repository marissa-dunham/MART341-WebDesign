Olympic National Park

Olympic National Park is on the Olympic Penninsula of Washington state in the Pacific Northwest.

This park was established in 1938 and is a total of 1,442 squared miles. There are four regions inside of Olympic National Park:

  1. Pacific coastline
  2. Alpine areas
  3. West-side temperate rainforest
  4. East-side drier forests
