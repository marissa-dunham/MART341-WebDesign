Types of augmentative and alternative communication devices:

Low-tech devices
High-tech devices
Augmentative and alternative communication (AAC) are ways individuals can communicate with others without using verbal speech. There are a wide range of devices someone can use, including low-tech devices and high tech devices.

Devices

Low-tech devices

Low-tech devices are systems that do not need to rely on technology. Examples of low-tech devices include communication boards/books, pictures and symbols, pen/paper, etc.

High-tech devices

High-tech devices are systems that do reoly on technollgy. Examples of high-tech devices include speech generating devices, tablets, eye gaze devices, etc.
